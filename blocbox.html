BLOCBOX

OVERVIEW

Blocbox is a could-based software I designed that allows users to save, share and collaborate information: create simple notes, store images, and add links.

PROBLEM

User’s found it frustrating to use current software services, currently active in the market, that allows them to organize & manage all kinds of information. Based on the intervieews, it was clear that current products weren’t user-focused and didnt offer an seamless user experience, and my role was to fix this.

SOLUTION

After following the guideline of my 8-step design process (Reseach, Goal, Storytelling, Branding, Prototype, Test, Iteration, and Validation), I was able to create Blocbox, an Saas product, that let users share and collect information with ease, and also include qualities that distinguishes from other typical book-marking features.

4 BRANDING

STYLE GUIDE

During the making of blocbox’s logo, typography, and color palette, I thought implementing the themes of minimalism & simplicity was a perfect fit for the brand’s reputation and culture. I wanted to create a professional brand, yet also include a tint of playfulness and a welcoming vibe.

Focusing on the theme of minimalism and simplicity, I thought including only the letter B inside a blue box, would be perfect. My goal at this point was to keep the tone of the brand logo, somewhat neutral at most, and let the content of blocbox do the talking.

The complimentary and opaque flavor of the color blue to me represents the essence of calmness. I simply wanted users to use blocbox with ease and gentle flow throughout the whole process.
