WALDEN

ABOUT

Walden is an e-commerce marketplace where consumers can view and purchase hand-made products created by artists and designers who are willing to donate 100% of their earnings to non-profit organizations that are in need of funding and donation.

PROBLEM

Consumers were able to fully empathize with Walden’s business philosophy and mission, yet found it difficult and frustrating to engage with their outdated and complicated web & mobile experience.

SOLUTION

Walden was able to refine its mobile and web experience, alleviate all of the pain points consumers went through, and completely rebrand their image for the purpose of increasing more engagement with future consumers.

UNDERSTANDING THE MAIN PROBLEM

Weak User-Centric Web & Mobile Design

Weak Engagement Between Walden & Consumers

Low Sales

Low Donation

Low Impact For Social Service

Low Recognition From Social Media + From New Potential Customers
