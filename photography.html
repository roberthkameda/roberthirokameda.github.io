How does Design and Photography relate?

  • creates an affectionate visual story all happening in one frame
  • visually transcend an idea to viewers, which in my case is to educate people to value "nature" and the "simple lifestyle" a bit more...basically to stop looking at the screen and go out, life's too short!! (ex. surfing, hiking, backpacking, traveling)
  • communicate emotionally & therapeautically with viewers who loves or needs art to refresh their mind.
  • gives an opportunity for audiences to observe, reflect, and ask questions (ex. What was that surfer girl's emotional status while walking along the beach? Did carrying her surfboard on top of her head help mitigate pain from her arm or hand injury she had a couple weeks ago? Or just tired from after work? Or just a clever way to save energy before paddling out? etc).
  • The questions that one comes up with is endless, and adding 4 or 5 more designers in a team can magnify the possibilities of how each perceived the meaning of the visual story, which I think is vital during collaborative interactions during real case projects in all sectors from social impact, healthcare, and service design.
